Why this course matters

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course focused on responsible, no-code AI tools for task automation, document creation, data analysis, research support, chatbot design, communication, and a final capstone showcase.

🎥 Weekly live workshops 🤝 Live help sessions 🌍 Private community forums 📝 Personalized feedback 🚀 Capstone coaching 🏅 Shareable certificate

Task automation through AI workflows, browser agents, and email or app-to-app automations.
